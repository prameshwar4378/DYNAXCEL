About Us

Dynaxcel, founded in 1995 by experienced engineers, is a pioneer in critical equipment design and manufacturing, committed to innovation and excellence across industries.

Dynaxcel: Pioneers in Critical Equipment Design and Manufacturing

Dynaxcel is a company started by young entrepreneurs in 1995. Promotor Directors Mr. Shailendra Mishra & Mr. Sandeep Deore are engineering graduates from renowned institutes with 29 years of experience in critical equipment design & manufacturing. Their thorough metallurgical and production expertise has led company to have strong engineering team and dedicated workforce, catering customers across sectors. With a 3000 sq ft workshop to begin with, Dynaxcel today has grown to 120000 sq ft land and 60500 sq ft area under the crane with modern facilities.
