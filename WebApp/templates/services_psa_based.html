Principle of Operation

The PSA plant employs Zeolite molecular sieves to separate oxygen from the air. High-purity oxygen is delivered while the nitrogen adsorbed by the molecular sieves is sent back into the air through the exhaust pipe. The PSA process comprises two vessels filled with molecular sieves and activated alumina.
