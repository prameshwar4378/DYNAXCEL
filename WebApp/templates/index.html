We Offer a Diverse Range of Services


Stainless Steel & Exotic Fabrication

We specialize in processing stainless steel. Specially trained workforce to handle stainless steel takes care of material by having dedicated area, spotless handling and dedicated toolset.

Explore More
ASME & PED Vessels

We are certified by Lloyds Register for ‘U’ stamp vessels and produce ‘PED’ vessels under the supervision of leading agencies like Bureau Veritas and TUV Rheinland.

Explore More
Railway & Metro Components

We have accreditations as per ISO3834-2 for welding and EN 15085-2 CL 1 for manufacturing metro components. We deal in steel, stainless steel, and aluminum metallurgy.

Explore More
Defence Equipment

We collaborate with leading equipment manufacturers in the defense sector, providing sub-assemblies for missile launch pad systems, missile cassette structures, and deflectors.

Explore More
Food & Pharma

Our equipment is produced in a dedicated stainless steel clean room to prevent contamination, and we achieve a polished finish of up to 0.4 Ra in-house, with Electro-Polishing performed by specialized vendors.

Explore More
PSA Based Oxygen Generator

The PSA plant employs Zeolite molecular sieves to separate oxygen from the air. The PSA process comprises two vessels filled with molecular sieves and activated alumina.
