Stainless Steel & Exotic Fabrication

We specialize in processing stainless steel. Specially trained workforce to handle stainless steel takes care of material by having dedicated areas, spotless handling, and dedicated toolset.

Exotic material is primarily cut by water-jet cutting since it is thermal sensitive metallurgy. Water Jet Cutting avoids any thermal impact on it.

For most of the equipment, we protect SS surface with PVC film to avoid contamination & the final surface treatment is done either by glass bead cleaning or pressure-wash cleaning.
